⚠️ Important Notes (Very Important for ESP32)

HC-SR04 Echo gives 5V output.
ESP32 works on 3.3V logic.
👉 Use a voltage divider (1kΩ + 2kΩ resistor) on every ECHO pin to protect ESP32.

If servo behaves unstable, use separate 5V external power supply for servo.

All GNDs must be connected together (common ground).
